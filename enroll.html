Choose a Payment Plan

Full year

Payments for an entire year (September-May) will receive a 5% discount on your overall tuition. One month's tuition will be due on September 1st and remaining balance must be paid in full by September 15th. Refunds will be available for classes dropped with 30 days notice, but already completed classes will be billed from the overall amount at the monthly rate.

Semester

Payment for an entire semester will receive a 2% discount on your overall tuition. One month's tuition will be due September 1st and remaining first semester balance must be paid by September 15th. Second semester tuition is due by January 1st.

Monthly

Pay by the month. Payments will be prorated for holiday months.
