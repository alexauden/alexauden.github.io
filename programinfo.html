Conservatory Program

The Conservatory Program is for students ages 8+, and is for students who have some knowledge of their instrument already and are interested in studying a conservatory curriculum. Students will attend weekly lessons, and will attend weekly masterclass sessions with their teacher and other students. These students will be required to participate in our three annual recitals and will have the option to perform for many other events during the year.


How do I sign up?

Interested students should sign up online using our online registration system. Students who are new to their instrument are reccomended to enroll in 1 trimester of a group class for their instrument. This sets the foundation for a solid technique in a fun environment where students can learn from each other and get to know others who play their instrument.
